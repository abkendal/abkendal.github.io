General Cooking

Use ¾ the amount of maple syrup as sugar is called for in the recipe.

Baking

For every cup of sugar in the recipe substitute ¾ - 1 ½ cups of maple syrup. Reduce the dominant liquid in the recipe by 2 – 4 tablespoons.
